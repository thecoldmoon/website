About.

Hi, I'm Lulu...

‘Multi-disciplinary’ would probably be the best word for it.


 I come from a creative background, as an digital and visual arts major in highschool and an active member of the academic and local art scenes in Toronto. I am fascinated by the human experience in its raw, naked form in whichever medium or means it may be expressed. My creative interests mostly venture towards film and filmmaking and theatre but I also like to dabble in design trends, art history and likewise.
 My transition towards engineering and the technological field came out of my interest and continuous strength performing in STEM courses in school. Or perhaps watching a lot of sci-fi films helped a whole bunch as well.
 What I loved about art was the ability to create something, to express an idea and to call it your own. I wondered what it would be like to take my creative background and create things practically in the context of stem: combining both loves in a sense. I’m interested in innovation and pushing the boundaries of technology to see what we are capable of achieving. Additionally, I love discussing about the ethics of technology :particularly in the context of AI.

 Currently, I’m on my second study term at the University of Waterloo for Systems Design Engineering. Feel free to reach out!
